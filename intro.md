# Designing Defensible Dissertation Research 🎓

**LIS 971 @ Dominican University | School of Information Studies**

---    

This interactive eBook is your methodological companion for **LIS 971: Research Methods for Dissertation Work**. It synthesizes the core readings, frameworks, and practical tools you need to design, defend, and write a rigorous methodology chapter for your dissertation.

## What This Course Is About

Unlike traditional research methods courses, LIS 971 recognizes that each of you is studying a different topic and may employ different methodological approaches. The goal is to ensure you develop **both** broad methodological literacy **and** deep expertise in the specific methods you'll use in your dissertation work.

This eBook mirrors the three-block course structure. Each block builds a component of your methodology chapter, and by the end, you'll have a complete, defensible chapter ready for your committee or IRB.

## How the Blocks Work

| Block | Topic | Deliverable | Dates |
|-------|-------|-------------|-------|
| 🟢 **Block 1** | Research Design + Sampling | 4–6 pp. research approach + sampling plan | Mar 16 – Mar 29 |
| 🔵 **Block 2** | Data Collection Protocols | Ready-to-implement instruments & protocols | Mar 30 – Apr 12 |
| 🟣 **Block 3** | Analysis Plan | 5–7 pp. analysis procedures | Apr 13 – Apr 26 |
| ⬛ **Final** | Methodology Chapter | 12–18 pp. complete chapter | May 9 |

## What You'll Find Here

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 📖 **Synthesized Theory** | Key concepts from your assigned readings, organized by block and decision point |
