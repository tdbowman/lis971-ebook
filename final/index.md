**April 27 – May 9 | 20% of course grade**

> *How do you synthesize all of this into a single, defensible chapter?*

## Overview

Your final deliverable is a **complete, polished methodology chapter** (or IRB-ready research protocol) that synthesizes and refines all your block deliverables. This should be dissertation-quality work that you can submit directly to your committee or IRB with minimal additional revision.

## Requirements

- **Length**: 12–18 pages (excluding references, appendices)
- **Format**: APA 7th edition throughout
- **Content**: Comprehensive integration of:
  1. Research design
  2. Sampling
  3. Data collection
  4. Analysis
  5. Validity/trustworthiness
  6. Ethical considerations
  7. Limitations
- **For mixed methods**: Clear integration strategy and rationale
- **Incorporates all feedback** received throughout the course
