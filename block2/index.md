# Block 2: Data Collection Protocols 🔵

**March 30 – April 12 | 20% of course grade**

---

> *How exactly will you gather your data?*

## Block Overview

Block 2 moves from *design* to *implementation*. You will develop complete, ready-to-implement protocols for all data collection procedures. These should be detailed enough that another researcher could replicate your study — a standard known as the "replication criterion."

## What You'll Learn

By the end of Block 2, you should be able to:

- Design rigorous data collection instruments appropriate to your methodology
- Develop detailed protocols that address validity and reliability
- Plan for data management, storage, and security
- Integrate ethical considerations into your data collection design

## Block 2 Deliverable

**Data Collection Protocols** (complete, ready-to-implement)

Depending on your methods, your deliverable must include:

1. Detailed procedures for all data collection methods
2. Validity/reliability considerations
3. Data management plan
4. Ethical considerations

**Method-specific requirements:**

| If You're Using... | Include... |
|---------------------|-----------|
| Interviews | Full interview guide with probes, opening/closing scripts |
| Surveys | Complete instrument with all items, scales, skip logic |
| Observations | Observation protocol, fieldnote template, schedule |
| Document analysis | Data extraction form, inclusion criteria, sampling plan |
| Digital trace data | Collection procedures, API specifications, ethical review |
