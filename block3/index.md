# Block 3: Analysis Plan 🟣

**April 13 – April 26 | 20% of course grade**

---

> *How exactly will you analyze your data, and how will you ensure quality?*

## Block Overview

Block 3 is where you specify, in precise detail, how you will move from raw data to findings. Your analysis plan should be specific enough that another researcher could follow your procedures and arrive at comparable results.

## What You'll Learn

By the end of Block 3, you should be able to:

- Specify appropriate analytic procedures for your research tradition
- Articulate quality criteria relevant to your methodology
- Design an integration strategy (for mixed methods researchers)
- Connect every analytic procedure back to your research questions

## Block 3 Deliverable

**Analysis Plan** (5–7 pages)

Method-specific requirements:

- **Qualitative students**: Coding approach, software, trustworthiness strategies
- **Quantitative students**: Statistical procedures, variable operationalization, power analysis
- **Mixed methods students**: Integration design matrix showing when/how data sources converge
- **All students**: Connection between analysis approach and research questions
